## The Seven principles of “da Vincian thinking” are: …

1.  Curiositá
2.  Dimostrazione
3.  Sensazione
4.  Sfumato
5.  Arte/Scienza
6.  Corporalita
7.  Connessione

These aren't mere thinking - It's a principled approach or an attitude.
